Niall is a London-based writer of novels, short stories, tweets and code.

He writes in C# for games, Javascript for websites and English for everything else. He recently got sick of printing his novels off and throwing them out windows and signed with Unbound.

Novels

My first novel, The Second Death of Daedalus Mole, is currently being crowdfunded for publication with Unbound. They're basically a normal publisher, but they fund books in advance through pre-orders (or 'pledges') and then split the profits 50-50 with the author.

It's a space opera set in our own galaxy a few hundred years in the future. It was longlisted for the inaugural Bath Novel Award. Find out more.

My second novel is top secret, but it's another space opera set some time earlier, and it's about a piece of stolen artwork that has dangerous implications.

Journalism

Much of my writing education came from writing for blogs and news sites about games. I used to write free pieces for Bitmob (including one of the first ever long-form articles on Minecraft!) as well as more artsy essays for The Clockwork Manual when I was a tiny baby man.

I have some new pieces brewing, expect to see some here in the near future.
