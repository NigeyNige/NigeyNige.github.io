Pizza Crash

A little prototype game about delivering pizzas. I made it mainly to learn about vehicle physics simulation and game flow.

It's available to try out on itch.io even if it is a buggy mess. Feel free to muck about with it and have fun!

It's coming along slowly, but there's already a lot of features in there like stealth mechanics, cool gadgets, Pokemon-Snap style photograph challenges and coded messages.
